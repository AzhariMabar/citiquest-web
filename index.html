About Us

CITIQUEST, the groundbreaking super app by BeOne Technology, merges the real world with the Metaverse, introducing an immersive gaming experience driven by blockchain technology. Players earn and utilize ONE tokens for transactions, quests, and personalization, unlocking rewards and accessing premium features. The game offers diverse quests, fosters virtual entrepreneurship, introduces virtual pets for companionship, and enables real-time exploration of a vibrant cityscape. With dynamic integration of businesses, transactions, and characters, CITIQUEST reshapes gaming, offering a transformative journey where every interaction shapes the evolving digital universe.
